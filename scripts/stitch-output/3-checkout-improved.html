Booking

How do I book?

expand_more
Simply click the "Book Now" button in the navigation bar. Select your preferred stylist, service, and time slot. Our GroomAI system will confirm your appointment instantly.

Can I cancel?

expand_more
Yes, you can cancel appointments up to 24 hours before the scheduled time through your Dashboard for a full refund.

How to reschedule?

expand_more
Rescheduling can be done via the "My Appointments" section. Choose a new time slot that fits your schedule, subject to stylist availability.
payments

Payment

What methods accepted?

expand_more
we accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and GroomAI Gold Credits available through our Black Label membership.

Is it secure?

expand_more
Absolutely. All transactions are encrypted with enterprise-grade SSL security and processed through industry-leading secure payment gateways.

Refund policy?

expand_more
Refunds are issued for cancellations made 24+ hours in advance. For service dissatisfaction, please contact our support within 48 hours of your appointment.

Account

How to sign up?

expand_more
Click the user icon in the top right corner and select "Join GroomAI". You can sign up using your email or social accounts.

Reset password?

expand_more
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to receive a secure reset link via your registered email address.
